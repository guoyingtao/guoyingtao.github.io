Frequently asked questions

Do I need an Apple Watch to use WristInk?
No. WristInk works great on iPhone alone. The Apple Watch app is a bonus that lets you practice writing characters on your wrist with haptic feedback.

What's included for free?
A starter deck of common characters, writing practice, flashcards, spaced repetition, and progress tracking are all free.

What does WristInk Pro unlock?
A one-time purchase unlocks the complete HSK 3.0 (新版) decks — levels 1–9, around 3,000 characters — along with the smart review algorithm. Yours to keep, forever.

Does WristInk work offline?
Yes. WristInk runs entirely on your device and needs no internet connection to practice.

Is my data collected?
No. WristInk doesn't collect personal information, doesn't track you, and keeps your practice data on your device. See our Privacy Policy for details.
