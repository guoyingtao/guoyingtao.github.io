LabelAI

Smart Image Labeling Powered by AI

LabelAI is an iOS app that uses powerful cloud-based artificial intelligence to recognize and label objects in your photos. Simply pick an image and let LabelAI identify what's inside — quickly and accurately.

Features

  • Advanced AI image recognition powered by leading cloud AI
  • Automatically detect and label objects in your photos
  • Fast, accurate results with a clean, easy-to-use interface
  • We don't store your photos on our servers

Frequently Asked Questions

How does LabelAI recognize images?
LabelAI sends the image you choose to a third-party cloud AI service (currently Google Gemini), which analyzes it and returns the labels to your device.

Why does the app need photo access?
LabelAI needs access to your photo library only so you can choose images to label.

Does LabelAI work offline?
No. Because recognition is performed by a cloud-based AI service, an internet connection is required.

Are my photos stored anywhere?
We do not store your photos on our own servers. Images are sent to a third-party AI service (currently Google Gemini) for processing and handled according to that provider's privacy policy. See the Google Privacy Policy for the current provider.
