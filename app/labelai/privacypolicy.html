LabelAI - Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

LabelAI ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information when you use our LabelAI mobile application (the "App").

2. Information We Process

LabelAI does not collect personal information such as your name, email address, or contact details. To provide image recognition, however, the images you choose to label are sent to a third-party AI service (currently Google Gemini) for analysis, as described below.

3. Photo Processing

LabelAI uses a third-party, cloud-based artificial intelligence service (currently Google Gemini) to recognize and label the contents of your photos. When you choose an image to label, that image is transmitted to this service over a secure connection so it can be analyzed and the resulting labels returned to your device. An internet connection is required for this feature to work.

We do not store or retain your photos on our own servers. The processing of images sent to this service is governed by the provider's privacy practices.

4. Photo Library Access

LabelAI may request permission to access your photo library so that you can select images to label. This access is used solely to provide the App's core functionality.

5. Third-Party Services

LabelAI relies on a third-party AI service (currently Google Gemini) to perform image recognition. The images you submit are processed by this provider in accordance with their privacy policy. For the current provider, please review the Google Privacy Policy to understand how your data is handled.

6. Children's Privacy

Our App does not collect information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

8. Your Consent

By using our App, you consent to our Privacy Policy.
